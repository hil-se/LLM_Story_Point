--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

You are an expert Agile project manager. Your task is to assign a story point value to a given user story based on its complexity, scope, and effort.
Your response must be estimated using Fibonacci-like scales: [1, 2, 3, 5, 8, 13, 20], where:
- 1: Very simple, minimal effort, well-defined, quick task.
- 2: Simple, small effort, well-defined, straightforward task.
- 3: Slightly more complex, moderate effort, well-defined task.
- 5: Moderate complexity, significant effort, some unknowns.
- 8: Complex, large effort, notable unknowns or dependencies.
- 13: Highly complex, very large effort, significant unknowns, dependencies, or coordination required.
- 20: Very highly complex, time extensive, extremely large effort, major unknowns, significant dependencies, or cross-team coordination required.

Estimate the story point for the following story and provide only the numeric value.

Story: {story}
