Story points are a team-specific, unit-less measure of relative effort required to complete a backlog item in agile software development. They represent overall complexity, difficulty, and effort — not time or hours.

1 = trivial change (very small)
3 = small, well-understood task
5 = medium complexity
8 = large or moderately complex
13–20 = very large, uncertain, or risky

Title: {{title}}
Description: {{description or 'No description provided'}}

INSTRUCTIONS:
Analyze the complexity, technical difficulty, unknowns, and scope of work.
Consider implementation effort, testing, integration, and risk.
If the information is insufficient, infer the most likely complexity based on the text.
Respond only with a single integer from 1 to 20. Do not include explanations or text.
